One more time, I could say this is my firstime feeling bad about my art (project). Here I just feel enslaved and nothing else to get appreciated. This is really bad, I couldn't stay for a long time anymore. So, I choose resign from this team. Leave all my codes (that I have built alone before) for them. everything that happened here I made it a lesson for my better future and better teamwork.
